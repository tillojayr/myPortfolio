How Far is too far?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is just the emulation of human intelligence in machines that are built to behave and think like humans. The phrase can also be used to refer to any computer that demonstrates characteristics of the human intellect, like learning and problem- solving. AI aims to imitate the human brain and develop systems that are intelligent and autonomous. The age of AI, according to some, has arrived, then why aren't there flying automobiles or robot maids bringing us lunch. AI is made to make you unaware that a machine is making decisions. It is anticipated that AI will soon become significantly more clever and a little less artificial. Naturally, AI will start acting like an intelligent worker as it gets smarter. We will still need human professionals to build and maintain these synthetic BOTS and concentrate on the strategic and creative components of a job, even though the majority of the repetitive and mostly time-oriented duties can be automated using AI.
