The Space Architect of Mars

Our planet is facing a historic climatic emergency, which is exacerbated by overpopulation and a food crisis. Is there a viable alternative that could solve these issues on its own?Some scientists believe that pushing the bounds of human civilisation to Mars and beyond is the only long-term solution.

Experts from NASA and Wageningen University have collaborated to create viable houses for human existence.How can we cultivate food that can endure cosmic radiation, and how will we build colonies on Mars' harsh surface? AI can determine the optimum shapes for our future Martian homes, where they should be put, and how to raise crops in space.

With enough data, seemingly intractable topics can be made more approachable by artificial intelligence. This movie, narrated by prominent actor and science enthusiast Robert Downey Jr., well known for his role as Iron Man, introduces cutting-edge technology that has the potential to revolutionize agricultural and space exploration.
