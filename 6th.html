Will Robot Take my Job?

Robots have revolutionized industrial manufacturing, and they are also being used in food production and in kitchens in restaurants. Artificial intelligence (AI) robots are already capable of doing a wide range of jobs that call for learning and judgment, including self-driving cars, insurance assessment, stock trading, accounting, human resource management, and many functions in healthcare. So, will there be no jobs in the future, or will there be new jobs to replace the ones that are lost?

The optimistic viewpoint claims that as automation continues to advance, it will lead to new job opportunities for individuals who have been laid off. There is some historical support for this: More than a century ago, people believed that the rise of the vehicle would be detrimental to the working class.
